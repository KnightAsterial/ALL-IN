Day 3 - Brain Breakdown

Students will learn how amyloid plaques and neurofibrillary tangles can damage the brain.

Dropping and tagging for science

In this lesson, students will learn about the hallmarks of Alzheimer's disease: Amyloid plaques and neurofibrillary tangles. Through two different games, kids will have fun while learning about complex processes in a brain with Alzheimer's disease. By playing a game called Neuron Drop, where students learn about how amyloid plaques are formed. They also play a tag type game to learn about neurofibrillary tangles.

Follow-Up Worksheet 3

This follow up worksheet gives kids the opportunity to further explore the topics from this lesson. They will have the option to make comparisions, do math, write a reaction, or write a story.
