Day 1 - The Healthy Brain

Students will learn about the parts of the healthy brain and their functions.

Meet the brain

Since our kit follows a story, the first day is the exposition. In this lesson, we expose the kids to the elements of the healthy brain like their own. At the end of the lesson, students should be able to identify and understand the functions of the various lobes of the brain. They'll go through stations to explore each function of the various brain lobes.

Fontal Lobe Student Instructions

The first lesson includes 5 stations. Here are the instruction sheets for the frontal lobe station, in case it is lost after the first use of the kit. For this station, students will solve critical thinking puzzles also known as rebus puzzles.

Temporal Lobe Student Instructions

Here are the instruction sheets for the temporal lobe station, in case it is lost after the first use of the kit. At this station, students will test their memory with memory cards. This game will be utilized again in the fourth lesson, but made harder with cards that are more difficult to match.

Parietal Lobe Student Instructions

Here are the instruction sheets for the parietal lobe station, in case it is lost after the first use of the kit. For this station, students will get to use their feelings of touch to identify ordinary classroom objects.

Occipital Lobe Student Instructions

Here are the instruction sheets for the occipital lobe station, in case it is lost after the first use of the kit. Here, students will look at visual puzzles and try and find objects hidden in pictures to test their visual senses.

Cerebellum Student Instructions

Here are the instructions for the Cerebellum station, in case it is lost after the first use of the kit. To learn about the cerebellum, students will test their balance with various physical challenges.

Follow-Up Worksheet 1

At the end of each lesson, teachers have the opportunity to assign either homework or reinforce the science lessons in class. Each worksheet in this kit includes 4 possible assignments for students to do, and each assignment on the worksheets are different. This worksheet includes vocab review, making connections, doing research, and creating art.
