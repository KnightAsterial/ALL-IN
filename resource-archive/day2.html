Day 2 - Neurons

Students will learn about the neuron and how they can be blocked by Alzheimer's Disease.

Tracks to teach

By utilizing tracks and marbles in this lesson, our goal is to teach students about how neurons send messages from the brain to the muscles. At the end of the lesson the students should understand what a neuron is, how Alzheimer's disease affects neurons, and how a message gets from place to place in the body. Our marble tracks have a very concrete analogy about each piece and what part of the nervous system represents.

Follow-Up Worksheet

After the lesson, the students will have an opportunity to excersize what they've learned. This worksheet includes story writing, math problems, concept mapping, and reading predictions.
