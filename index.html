Our kit emphasizes

Interactivity

While existing kits rely primarily on direct instruction, where the teacher uses a model only to demonstrate a concept to the class, our kit was designed to be an hands-on, immersive experience for students. This method better engages the students and leads to a higher understanding of the content.

Emotional Engagement

Emotions are one of the most powerful motivators, so we hope to give our students a deep emotional connection with an Alzheimer's patient and his family. Our kit follows the story of Albert Z. Heimer as he is diagnosed with Alzheimer's and has to deal with the emotional turmoil that follows.

Ease of Use

All of the instructional material and worksheets included in our kit are available online. Never again will teachers need to worry about losing their hardcopies. Our teacher manual was also written to be concise and detailed, so it is easily understandable for new teachers.

Scalability

While our kit is targeted towards third to fifth graders, our entire kit is modular and purchasable in parts. Various aspects of our kit can be used for education in doctor's offices, high schools, and everywhere in between.
